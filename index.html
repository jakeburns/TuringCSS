Our CSS Design

Overall Theme: Club Penguin

For our turing CSS design, we aimed to implement a clubpenguin theme in honor of the now dead clubpenguin.com.

Our palette (seen below) captures the essence of the overall theme clubpenguin used along with other colors we generated that fit nicely into the theme.

We implemented features such as:

  • Custom navigation bar with color change when hovering
  • Custom buttons that "animates" and changes color when hovered and clicked
  • seen on the my account page and when submitting preps or labs
  • Current days assignments are hilighted in green
  • Grades recieved for preps, labs, and homework are bold and red
  • MyAccount page formatted nicely

More specific to our theme we implemented:

  • Custom images for prep, grade, lab, and reading
  • Custom Club Penguin font
  • Modeled after clubpenguin homepage and its various shades of blue
